What We Do

Metadata Analysis & Resolution

We focus on identifying inconsistencies in music metadata that may impact rights recognition and royalty allocation.

🔍

ISRC & Catalog Inconsistencies

Detection of mismatched, missing, or duplicated ISRCs across public catalog listings and registration databases.

📊

DSP × Registry Cross-Reference

Cross-referencing public DSP metadata (e.g. Spotify) with registration datasets to surface discrepancies that may affect royalty routing.

⚠

Registration Gap Identification

Identification of potential gaps in rights registration where works may be generating revenue without proper claimant records.

📄

Structured Reporting

Structured, evidence-based reporting for publishers and rights holders with clear documentation of findings and recommended actions.

Internal Tooling

Our Platforms

Our internal platforms support analysis and workflow execution.

VerseIQ

Catalog comparison engine that cross-references public streaming metadata against known registration records.

TrapRoyaltiesPro

Registration audit layer that identifies potential gaps in performer and rights holder registrations at collection societies.

SMPT / Roya

Metadata structuring tools that normalize and verify data formats for consistency across international rights systems.
