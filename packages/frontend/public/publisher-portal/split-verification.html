Verify writer splits before they hit a PRO

Split Verification is a focused tool for the most common registration failure: two writers, three percentages, no agreement. It normalizes contributor names, checks that shares total 100%, flags conflicting entries, and lets co-writers sign off on the final split.

Typical issues it catches

  • A 50/50 work where one row reads 60% and the other 50% (total 110%)
  • Writer "JOHN SMITH" and "John Smith" treated as different contributors
  • Publisher share missing — writer collects 100%, publisher collects nothing
  • Non-composer/lyricist/writer roles holding writer shares
  • Foreign co-writers without an affiliated society on file

How the workflow runs

Single-work or bulk

Paste one work, upload a CSV of many, or connect your catalog export. The tool runs the same rules either way.

One-click fixes

Normalize names, redistribute shares to 100%, merge duplicate contributors. Every fix is staged — you commit when you're ready.

Inline sign-off

Optional co-writer email flow: they approve the final split by link, no login required. Timestamped audit trail per work.

Export to CWR

Verified works export cleanly into CWR v2.1 packets or straight into your DSP metadata pipeline.
