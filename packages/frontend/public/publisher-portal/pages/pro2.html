PRO Pilot — 4 weeks

A focused pilot to evaluate how pre-validation of metadata can contribute to improved data quality before registration with PRO.
📄 PRO Documentation →
📦 Scope
20–50 works from one publisher · No integration required · Controlled test environment
📅 Implementation
Week 1
Baseline Analysis
  • Review of existing material
  • Identification of metadata deviations
  • Initial analysis summary
Week 2
Validation
  • Structured review of identified deviations
  • Publisher reviews and approves adjustments
  • Documentation of validation process
Week 3
Comparative Analysis
  • Comparison of original and validated material
  • Focus on data quality and handling process
Week 4
Summary
  • Overall analysis of results
  • Identification of potential improvements
  • Joint review
🤝 Purpose

To evaluate how a pre-validation process can contribute to:

  • Improved data quality
  • Reduced manual handling
  • Clearer documentation for registration
⚠️ Important: The pilot aims to analyze and understand effects — not to replace existing processes or systems.
