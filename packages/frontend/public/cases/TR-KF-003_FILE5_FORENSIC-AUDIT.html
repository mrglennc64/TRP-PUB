5. Regulatory Standing & Statutory Basis
5.1 Music Modernization Act (17 U.S.C. §114)

Unmatched royalties remain in Suspense until a valid LOD and Schedule 1 are filed. The three-year retroactive recovery window applies to all accruals within the 36-month lookback period.

5.2 Featured Performer LOD Requirement

Labels do not file performer LODs. The performer must independently file via Search & Claim or LOD Part 1 directly with SoundExchange.
